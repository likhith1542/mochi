Meet Mochi 🐾

A tiny pixel pet that lives on top of your screen — and gently reminds you to drink water, rest your eyes, and make it to your meetings.

click her to say hi ♥

She's not in your way. She's on your side.

A transparent overlay with real physics — your clicks pass straight through to your apps, everywhere except on her.

🫟

Never blocks a click

She walks over every window, but the overlay is click-through everywhere except her tiny body. Your work stays yours.

🪀

Real physics

Drag her, toss her, watch her bounce and land with a squash. Release her gently mid-air and she stays pinned right there.

🪟

Sits on your windows

Drop her onto your focused window and she'll perch on its top edge, stroll along it, and tumble off the end.

💧

Gentle reminders

Water, 20-20-20 eye breaks, stretching — or anything you type, repeating or one-shot. She acts out each reminder: drinking, reading, standing tall.

📅

Calendar alerts

Paste your Google or Apple calendar's private iCal link and she'll hop with a speech bubble minutes before each event.

🎧

Full of life

She naps, blinks, follows your cursor, wanders over when your mouse rests, purrs when petted — and each species has its own voice.

29 pets, 7 species

Cats, puppies, bunnies, birds, ghosts — and a crew of kids in every skin tone. Each pet has its own name and voice. Click one to try it above.
